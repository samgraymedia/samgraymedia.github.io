Best Practices

  • Start with recent generations and work backwards
  • Keep detailed notes of your sources and findings
  • Cross-reference birth and death dates for accuracy
  • Consider spelling variations and name changes

Common Challenges

  • Names may be recorded differently than expected
  • Some records may not yet be transcribed
  • Birth dates on death records may be approximate
  • Multiple people may have the same name and dates
